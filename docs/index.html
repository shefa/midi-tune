Midi Tune

Real-time error correction and performance aid for MIDI instruments demo.

Just plug in your MIDI Keyboard and enjoy playing piano with the occasional aid of AI

(you can also use the sustain pedal)

While playing, the AI will try to correct your errors or occasionally play something that it finds more fitting :)

The longer you play, the better it will adapt to your style!

Built with magenta.js and tensorflow.js.
